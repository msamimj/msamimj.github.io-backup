FAQ

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q. What is a hackathon?

Ans. Hackathon, also known as the HACK DAY, HACKFEST or CODEFEST, are renowned events where in people holding expertise in different fields from computer programming to graphic designing work in harmony on software projects.

Q. What is NITS::HACKS 1.0?

NITS::HACKS 1.0 is a scheduled 24-hour Hackathon with contestants requiring to visualize and build solutions for disaster management/mitigation.

Q. Are there any travel reimbursements in NITS::HACKS 1.0?

Unfortunately, we are not able to provide travel reimbursements to participants. But we’re always on the hunt to find more sustainable ways to bring more students to hackathon.

Q. What if I don’t know how to code?

Not a problem! Our entire team knew very little when they each attended their very first hackathon. It’s entirely irrelevant what your experience is going into a hackathon, it’s more about your interest in technology. Even we are passionate about making our hackathon welcoming & beginner-friendly.

Q. Will I annoy people if I ask them questions?

Not at all! This is a common misconception about hackathons and in actual fact, it’s the complete opposite. The environment at these events are designed specifically to encourage everyone to have fun and help each other. If you’re ever stuck on anything, go find a volunteer who might able to help and if not, they will almost certainly know who can.

Q. Will there be food?

Just like any other traditional Hackathon, ENVISION would also provide pizzas and energy drinks to its contestants to sustain the 24-hour long brainstorming.

HOW TO
REACH

National Institute of Technology, Silchar is located just on the outskirts of the city of Silchar and is well connected to other parts of the country by a good network of road, rail or air. The distance of Silchar from the economic hub of the state, Guwahati is only 326 km. The major ways to reach us:

AIR: There are daily direct flights to Silchar from cities like Kolkata, Guwahati and Imphal. Air India and Jet Airways operate flights from Kolkata to Silchar/Guwahati. From airport, the Institute campus is approximately 35 Kms.

RAIL: Silchar has a direct rail link with Lumding/Guwahati. Many daily train services run between Silchar and Lumding. The campus is around 10 KMs from the Silchar railway station.

ROAD: Silchar is well connected with various cities through road. A 326 km long National Highway directly connects Silchar with Guwahati. A large number of day/night bus ply between Guwahati and Silchar in the morning and evening everyday.
