START UP BUZZ

Description


 With entrepreneurial roots seeping deeper into India's ecosystem, We introduce one more event START UP BUZZ. This event is relatively new and intends to familiarise you with the blooming start up culture in India.
Yes, you heard it right. If you have any start up idea or even if you don’t, don’t let go of this opportunity. Come prepared with a presentation illustrating your idea with all the details like start up cost, marketing, advertisement etc and present it in the most effective way.

Description

TASKABET

Description


 What’s better than a friendly game of scrabble? A 3-d scrabble where the player has to earn his or her alphabets. This is where Taskabet comes in ,our very own version of the popular board game. Perform the assigned tasks, win letters and make up words to earn points. The tasks may be physical or mental. You are free to play in groups of three or individually.

PIXZONA

Description


 Pixzona is a themed and time bound photography contest. Every participant will be required to capture a single photo which , according to him or her, clearly depicts the theme within the allotted time limit. Give us a glimpse of the budding photographer in you by showing us what you perceive through your lens. Remember it will be an on the spot photography contest.

WHAT IF?

Description


 What if the partition of India hadn’t happened ?What if the Americans hadn’t bombed Nagasaki or Neil Armstrong hadn’t successfully landed on the moon? All these predicaments and many other, the world surely would have been way different from how it is today. Let your mind drift off to the past days and change it the way you want. Choose an important event , change its direction and write how things would have been different because of it within the allotted time. Stop worrying about the present or future for a while as this is your only chance of redoing history.

XENG

Description


 School Genius' XENG , the quiz competion, gets a theme this year. All the budding astronomers and space enthusiasts, here is your chance to flaunt your grasp on the space.
The two-tiered competion will a pen paper prelims where each team has to complete the given set of questions within the allotted time. The qualifying teams will move on to the final round where they have to battle against time for the title. The final round questions will be aided by audio-visuals as well.

AD-MATINEE

Description


 AD-MATINEE is an advertisement making competition where you will have to make an ad of any product of your choice from the mentioned categories.
The categories are :-
a) Stationeries
b) Automobiles
c) Electronic gadgets
You can advertise any imaginary product or idea. Show your creativity through your ad.

Scientific Dig

Description


 Science has an appeal which surpasses every other field. This event is basically a low key science competition where the participants have to identify various background scientific phenomenon going on in the question asked. Questions will be aided by visuals.
