RoboWars

Description

Task / Problem Statement

The battle of bots will let you sink in the hue of warfare shaded with sparks of perfection.. Keep calm! More details to be here soon! :)

Specifications


Striker bot: The dimensions of the striker bot should not exceed 30cm*30cm*20cm (length*breadth*height) including the shooter, guider and wheels.

Rules

  • The striker bot can have shooting mechanism with it. And the guider bot will have only guider with it that can guide the ball throughout.
  • One player will control the striker bot and one player will control the guider bot.
  • No bot is allowed to pick the ball up or grip it or incorporate the ball within its body.
  • Participants are not permitted to touch the bot while the match is running unless it gets immobile for 90 seconds.
  • 3 minutes of repair time will be given. If a team is unable to repair it is liable to be disqualified.
  • The bot should not damage the arena.

    • Prices upto 30,000
