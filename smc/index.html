About Us

A car is one of the biggest consumer purchases which a person makes. Since time and age, buying a car has always been a time consuming process with an extra load of exhaustion that comes from all the hopping from one showroom to another hoping to find the perfect Car and the perfect deal.

ShopMyCar has been established with the sole purpose of making this car purchasing process seamless and sweat-free through which one can simply sit down comfortably and hop from one dealer to another to find the perfect Car and deal while just sitting in the living room. It comes with a single window experience from finding the most suitable car to choosing the right dealer, booking a test drive to finalizing the best deal available.

All of this has been made possible with the use of innovative technology to provide a customer friendly platform which is just a click away.
